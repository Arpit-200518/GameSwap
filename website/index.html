Frequently Asked Questions

How does buying games with NFTs work?
When you purchase a game on GameSwap, you receive a unique NFT that represents your ownership of the game. This NFT is stored in your digital wallet and can be used to download and play the game, as well as resell it in the future if you choose.
Is it safe to sell my games on GameSwap?
Our platform uses blockchain technology to ensure secure transactions. When you list a game for sale, it's protected by smart contracts, and the transfer of ownership only occurs when the payment is confirmed.
Can I trade games with other users?
Yes, GameSwap supports peer-to-peer trading. You can propose trades with other users, exchanging games in your collection for games in theirs, all secured by our blockchain technology.
